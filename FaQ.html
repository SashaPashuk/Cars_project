FAQ

What is the minimum age for renting a car?

The minimum age to rent a car in Dubai is 21 years old. If you have already reached this age, everything is in order, contact Kings Rental Cars to rent a car.

Is it enough for me to have a Russian driving license in Dubai?.

Yes, enough. You need the internal rights of the country of which you are a citizen - Russia, Ukraine, etc.

The trip failed, the car was booked. Will I get my money back?

We do not take prepayment for car rental. You pay for our services on the spot after arriving in Dubai. Therefore, if your trip is interrupted, please let us know and we will cancel your booking. Since the advance payment for car rental is not taken, there is no problem with the return of money.

What does car insurance cover?

If the accident occurred through the fault of the client, then the insurance will be 10% of the damage. Added to this is the fixed amount that the insurance company has set for the vehicle. This amount depends on the specific car brand. For example, for the Lamborghini Huracan it is $ 1370.

I had an accident. What to do?

The most important thing is to contact the landlord as soon as possible and tell them what happened. Our company's technical support representatives will promptly take the necessary measures and tell you how to behave depending on your specific circumstances. Remember not to leave the scene of the accident until the police have completed the paperwork.

What do you need to rent a car?

Kings Rental Cars requires 21 years of age or older to rent a car. You will need the following documents:

• original passport;

• an internal driver's license issued in the country of which you are a citizen;

• a bank card with enough money to pay for the rental services - the required amount will be blocked as a deposit, and then debited from your account.

How do I pay for my car rental?

You yourself choose which payment option is more convenient for you - by credit card or in cash. But keep in mind that when paying by card, the processing company additionally takes interest for using the money transfer service.

Are there traffic jams in Dubai?

Yes. In Dubai, on average, one car is shared by two people. Therefore, it is not surprising that traffic jams occur on the roads. Local authorities are fighting them, and the situation is gradually improving, but the problem has not yet been fully resolved. The most serious traffic jams are considered on the highway connecting Dubai with the emirate of Sharjah.

What kind of gasoline to fill in the car?

There are two types of gasoline on Dubai gasoline stations - Super (98) and Special (95). An employee at Kings Rental Cfrs will advise you which one should be put into the tank of your rented car. At the gas station there is no need to get out of the car, just tell the tanker the brand of gasoline you need and how many liters you need to fill in the tank.

Is smoking allowed in the car?

No. This is not only discouraged, but also punishable by a fine.

Is it allowed to carry alcoholic drinks in the rented car?

UAE law only allows the carriage of alcoholic beverages in a vehicle if you have the appropriate license. If you are carrying a small amount of alcohol, especially bought in Duty Free, for your own consumption, the police will treat you loyally. But the transportation of alcohol in such an amount that it is immediately obvious that this is a commercial batch will entail serious consequences not only for the driver, but also for the rental company.
